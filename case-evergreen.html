Evergreen Employee Onboarding Experience

An end-to-end onboarding solution designed to help newly hired service technicians understand the organization, navigate Evergreen Academy, meet customer service expectations, and apply safety and compliance practices through scenario-based learning.

Project Overview

Building a complete onboarding pathway.

This project introduces new employees to Evergreen Home Services through a blended eLearning experience. The course combines short instructional content, narrated onboarding moments, guided exploration, and authentic workplace scenarios centered on a fictional learner named Marcus Johnson.

Design Focus

From orientation to application.

The experience is being designed to move beyond passive onboarding. Learners explore company expectations, use a simulated LMS, locate resources, respond to training messages, and demonstrate understanding through practical scenario-based tasks.

Learning Experience

What the project includes.

Rise 360 Course Structure

Modules introduce the organization, employee support teams, Evergreen Academy, customer service expectations, safety responsibilities, and final scenario practice.

Storyline 360 LMS Simulation

Learners complete realistic LMS tasks, including enrolling in role-based training, locating archived course materials, responding to a message, and downloading a completion certificate.

Scenario-Based Assessment

Marcus Johnson provides continuity across the course, giving learners opportunities to interpret situations and apply onboarding knowledge in practical ways.
