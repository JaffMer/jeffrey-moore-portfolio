Case Study

Digital Media Production Pathway

Building digital literacy, collaboration, creativity, and project management through scaffolded podcast development.

Digital Learning · Project-Based Learning
← Return to Portfolio
AudienceGeneral music learners
TimelineApproximately 3 weeks
RoleLearning designer & facilitator
DeliverablesGuided tutorial, planning guide, rubric, peer review

Learning Challenge

The Problem

Learners were given access to a complex digital creation platform but lacked the guidance, structure, and foundational skills needed to use it effectively. Initial open exploration produced limited engagement and inconsistent outputs.

Solution

What I Designed

I redesigned the experience into a scaffolded three-week pathway. Learners moved from digital music concepts to guided platform exploration, remix practice, podcast planning, collaborative production, milestone check-ins, peer review, and final reflection.

Design decisions

The Choices Behind the Work

Progressive Scaffolding

Learners built confidence through increasingly complex tasks before creating a full podcast.

Collaboration By Design

Students worked in pairs and could interview one another or an outside subject matter expert.

Peer Review

Learners evaluated peer work against standards while reflecting on their own podcast quality.

Results & impact

What Changed

  • Learners progressed from novice platform users to collaborative digital creators
  • Milestones supported project management and pacing
  • Rubrics clarified expectations and strengthened final outputs
  • Peer review reinforced quality standards before submission

Reflection

The strongest design decision was creating a structured pathway instead of relying on a platform tutorial alone. Learners needed guided practice, checkpoints, and feedback systems before they could produce meaningful creative work independently.
